VIJAY P. BHATKAR

Computer Scientist, Information Technology Leader and Educationist

11 October 1946 (age 74)

Vijay Bhatkar is a computer scientist, information technology leader and educationist from India. He is known for leading the development of India's PARAM supercomputer . He has been awarded Padma Bhushan and Padma Shri by the Government of India while the Government of Maharashtra has honored him with Maharashtra Bhushan.

  • C-DACache Founder and First Executive Director
  • Programme and Lexicon International School, Madatnis and Salagar
  • Government of India Member of Classical Salagar Committee
  • Member of Amrita Vidyapeeth
  • Chairman of Dishnet (DSL)
  • International Salagar Board Member
  • Bharatatil Leading Technologist
  • Fellow - Computer Society of India (CSI)
  • Fellow - IEEE
  • Fellow - INE(Indian National Academy of Engineering)
  • Fellow -National Academy of Sciences, India
  • Fellow -Maharashtra Academy of Sciences
  • Fellow - Gokhale Educational Institution
  • Member -Working Group on R&D and HRD of IIT Task Force
